About D4AI

Our Story

D4AI focuses on promoting Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and providing data science training in Africa. We aim to empower individuals, organizations, and communities across the continent with data-driven skills and solutions to drive positive social impact and sustainable development.

Through our programs and impactful initiatives, we seek to bridge the digital divide, foster data literacy, and address pressing challenges faced by African communities.

By harnessing the power of data, we are dedicated to creating a more inclusive and prosperous future for Africa, where data-driven solutions drive positive change and innovation.
