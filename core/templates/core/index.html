We were founded to harness the power of data science to drive positive change and sustainable development in Africa

D4AI focuses on promoting Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and providing data science training in Africa. We aim to empower individuals, organizations, and communities across the continent with data-driven skills and solutions to drive positive social impact and sustainable development.

Through our programs and impactful initiatives, we seek to bridge the digital divide, foster data literacy, and address pressing challenges faced by African communities.

By harnessing the power of data, we are dedicated to creating a more inclusive and prosperous future for Africa, where data-driven solutions drive positive change and innovation.

Our Impact

Created an inclusive environment where women can effectively leverage data, break barriers, and catalyze meaningful change. Our goal is to empower women to embrace new opportunities, challenge norms, and make a lasting impact through the transformative power of data.

Built a strong and collaborative network that leverages data science expertise to address real-world challenges. By bringing together talented individuals and providing them with the necessary resources and support, we empower this community to make a tangible difference in various domains.

By unlocking the potential of data, we gave individuals and organizations the tools, knowledge, and resources to harness the full potential of their data by enabling them to make informed decisions, gain valuable insights, and drive innovation.
